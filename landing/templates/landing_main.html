Design

Any product that needs a manual to work is broken. Elon Musk

Here is a chance to showcase your desigining skills

$Design is an UI UX designing competition. The competitors would be provided with a real world problem on the spot. Participants would design a UI UX explaining their idea of the apps or websites working for the problem.

The top three designs selected by the panelist get an opportunity for a paid internship with Design Innovation Centre, University of Delhi.
