OSSOME

Software is like sex: it's better when it's free. Linus Torvalds

Participants will be required to contribute to some open source projects owned or maintained by our team members hosted on GitHub. Every issue (bug-fix, feature, docs, etc.) has an associated score. Score will be awarded at the discretion of the project maintainers. The top cumulative scoring participants will qualify for the on-site finals, where more projects and issues will be revealed.

Some profound quote

If you are a master story teller at heart and a data enthusiast by passion. DATAWEAVE is THE event for you,Register online and show your data prowess in a first of its kind DATA SCIENCE competition.

It is a two tier event

First round is an online Machine Learning competition, you need to solve a data problem. If the accuracy of your predictions is among the top 30 participants. We will invite you for a second onsite hackathon.

Second round will be a day long data science hackathon where you will work on a real world data problem and present your findings to a panel of expert judges.
